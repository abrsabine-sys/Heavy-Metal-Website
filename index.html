Origins of Heavy Metal

Heavy metal music originated in the late 1960s and early 1970s, primarily in the United Kingdom and the United States. Bands like Black Sabbath, Led Zeppelin, and Deep Purple are considered pioneers of the genre.

The music is known for its loud, distorted guitars, powerful vocals, and driving rhythms.

Key Characteristics

  • Distorted electric guitars
  • Strong bass lines
  • Emphatic drumming
  • Vocal styles ranging from melodic to aggressive
