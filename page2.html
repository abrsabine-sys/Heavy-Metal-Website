Heavy Metal Subgenres

Diverse Sounds Across Decades

Popular Subgenres

  • Thrash Metal
  • Death Metal
  • Black Metal
  • Power Metal

Each subgenre has its own style, image, and fan base. For example, thrash metal is fast and aggressive, while power metal often has melodic vocals and fantasy-themed lyrics.

Influential Bands by Subgenre

  1. Thrash: Metallica, Slayer
  2. Death: Death, Morbid Angel
  3. Black: Mayhem, Darkthrone
  4. Power: Helloween, DragonForce

Fun Fact

Heavy metal has inspired fashion, movies, video games, and even its own festivals, such as Wacken Open Air in Germany, which attracts fans from around the world.
